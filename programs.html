Programs

Kids in ICT

Computer science curriculum tailored to young children that makes coding easy and fun to learn. This program caters to 3 age groups:

  • 6 years to 9 years
  • 10 years to 14
  • 15 years to 17 years

High Schools Program

This program have partnered with Pascap Trust They run various programs supporting the education & wellbeing of pupils in the Western Cape Province. Together with Younglings Africa, we will be offering free ICT training to pupils (Grade 9 to Grade 11) they're serving within the Cape Flats communities as of April 2023. The program will be facilitated by one of our amazingly ambitious candidates, Mzwamadoda Shaun Louw, under Mihlali Dzingwa supervision. We will jointly host CapeFlats High Schools Hackathon twice every year (June and November) and have all the schools in our database compete for a prize.

Adults and Non Matriculants

This program provides candidates from false bay collage who have completed a NCV in ICT . We train them with coding skills such as HTML5 ,CSS, Javascript , Bootstrap 5 ,GIT ,GITHUB and Netlify . We give them practical experience to work around IT projects and build them Resumes.
